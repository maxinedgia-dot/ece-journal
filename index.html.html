ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Maxine Juliana S. De Guia, born on October 13, 2006, and currently pursuing a degree in Electronics Engineering at the University of Santo Tomas.

My academic journey began at Mind Care and Development Academy Incorporated, where I finished my elementary education. I continued my studies at the University of Rizal System for Junior High School and La Salle College Antipolo for Senior High School. Each stage of my education helped shape who I am: curious, hardworking, and eager to learn how technology connects people and ideas.

I'm deeply interested in cloud computing, and my ultimate goal is to become a Cloud Engineer. I'm fascinated by how massive digital systems work behind the scenes—how data moves, scales, and powers the online world. I'm also actively improving my programming skills, time management, and technical communication, knowing these will be vital in my future career.

Outside academics, I enjoy reading books to recharge my mind and playing computer games to sharpen my strategic thinking and problem-solving skills that translate surprisingly well into engineering!

In the long term, I aspire to build a career in cloud infrastructure and artificial intelligence, creating innovative systems that help shape the future of technology.
